Welcome to the Animal Gallery

Lion - A powerful big cat known as the "king of the jungle," with a majestic mane and a loud roar.

Elephant - The largest land animal, recognized for its long trunk, large ears, and intelligence.

Tiger - A striped big cat with sharp hunting skills, known for its strength and stealth.

Giraffe - The tallest land animal, with a long neck and spotted coat, reaching high tree leaves.

Zebra - A horse-like animal with black-and-white stripes, known for its speed and herd behavior.
